Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Candelas Music ("we", "us", or "our"), a solo artist based in Las Vegas, NV, collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

Newsletter Signup

When you sign up for our newsletter, we collect your email address. We use this to send you a welcome email and periodic updates (at most one email per week) regarding new music, video premieres, and tour dates.

Contact Form

When you use our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. This information is used solely to respond directly to your inquiry. We do not use contact form information for automated marketing or email campaigns.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with service providers who help us operate our website and newsletter (such as email service providers), but only to the extent necessary for them to provide those services.

Your Choices

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email we send you.
